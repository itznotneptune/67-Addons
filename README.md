67 Addons is a [ChatTrigger](https://chattriggers.com) module for Minecraft 1.8.9, designed to reduce the number of Chat Trigger modules you need to use.

---

## ‚ú® Features

> **Note:** This list is not the most updated and won't provide the most info on the module

### General

* **Auto Refill**: Automatically refill your inventory with pearls, jerries, or SuperBoom tnt!
* **Rag Axe Message**: Let your team know you got touched by mobs and that your rag axe was cancelled
* **Auto Equip Mask**: Automatically equip a mask when your health is low (<40%) // Not recommended since this will send weird packets
* **RNG Sound**: Plays a sound when you drop RNGs (doesn't work for dungeon or kuudra drops)

### Dungeons

* **Blood Rush Splits**: Tells you how long it took to clear each room on blood rush if you are archer or mage, also an option to change to make this work on every class
* **Crystal Alert**: Alerts you and displays a title on screen when you have a Crystal (Not movable)
* **Crystal Message**: Alerts your party members in case they're blind when you pick up or place down a Crystal
* **Rapid Fire Message**: Sends a message when you use Archer ult
* **Explo Shot Message**: Sends a message telling your party members how much damage per enemy you dealt with your Explo Shot ability
* **Hide Players After Leap**: Hides the player that you lept to after leaping
* **Score Milestones**: Sends a message when 270 and 300 score
* **Force Paul**: Forces +10 dungeon score for Paul
* **Announce Crypts**: Announces 5/5 crypts when all crypts are killed
* **Core Timestamps**: Sends Last player enter core time
* **Leap Message**: Sends a message after leaping to someone (default: class + name)
* **Shitter List**: Pretty self explanitory, do /shitter for help with this module enabled
* **Leap Counter**: Currently doesn't work properly
* **Block Wrong Relic Clicks**: Prevents you from clicking on the wrong relic cauldron and dying
* **Highlight Correct Cauldron**: Highlights the corresponding cauldron to the relic you picked up

### Commands

* **Party Chat Dungeon Queue Command**: Allows you to type the floor in chat to queue into a dungeon. Example: ff7, mm7

## üíæ Installation

1. Make sure you have **[ChatTriggers](https://chattriggers.com)** installed.
2. Download the latest release of 67 Addons from the [**Releases Page**](https://github.com/itznotneptune/67-Addons/releases).
3. Place the downloaded `.zip` file into your `config > ChatTriggers > modules` folder, located in your `.minecraft` directory.
4. Launch Minecraft, or if your game is already launched, do /ct load.
